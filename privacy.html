1. Who We Are

Motorway Monarch is a hobby project created to help motorway enthusiasts track their travels across Britain's motorway network. This service is operated independently and is not affiliated with any government agency or commercial entity.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address: Required for account creation and authentication
  • Username: Your chosen display name (3-20 characters)
  • Password: Securely hashed and stored by Firebase Authentication (we never see your actual password)
  • Motorway Visit Data: Which motorways you've marked as visited
  • Location (Optional): County/region you select in your profile
  • Favourite Motorway (Optional): Your selected favourite motorway

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Authentication Cookies: Firebase uses cookies to keep you logged in
  • Timestamps: When you created your account and last updated your data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for these purposes only:

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your user account
  • Core Functionality: Track which motorways you've visited
  • Leaderboard Display: Show your username, visit count, and miles on the public leaderboard
  • Profile Viewing: Allow other logged-in users to see your profile (username, location, favourite motorway, visit stats)
  • Authentication: Keep you securely logged in across sessions

4. What Information Is Public

4.1 Visible to Everyone (Even Logged Out)

  • Your username
  • Number of motorways visited
  • Total miles traveled
  • Completion percentage

4.2 Visible Only to Logged-In Users

  • Your location (if you added it)
  • Your favourite motorway (if you selected one)
  • Full profile details

4.3 Never Public

  • Your email address
  • Your password
  • When you created your account

8. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 Right to Access

You can view all your data in the "My Account" section. For a full data export, contact us.

8.2 Right to Rectification

You can update your username, location, and favourite motorway anytime in "My Account".

8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

Contact us to delete your account. We'll remove all your data within 30 days.

8.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You can stop using the service at any time by logging out and not logging back in.

8.5 Right to Data Portability

Contact us for a machine-readable export of your data (JSON format).

8.6 Right to Object

You can object to your data being displayed on the leaderboard by deleting your account.

8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
